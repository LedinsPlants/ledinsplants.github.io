Kohleria 'AL's Valborg'

Released asKohleria 'AL's Valborg'
Working nameAS-BR-001
ParentageKohleria 'Astarte' × 'Brimstone'
Cross pollination2017
Seeds harvested2017
Seeds sown2019
First bloom2020
Produces pollen?No
Still in cultivation?Yes (2026)

Notes

Kohleria 'AL's Valborg' is a plant from my first sucessful Kohleria cross. Since the seed parent, Kohleria 'Astarte' doesn't make pollen, this plant does also not make any pollen unfortunately. It has inherited a compact growth habit and silvery leaves from its pollen parent K. 'Brimstone'. As of 2026, I no longer grow this plant myself, although I know it is still in cultivation by at least a couple of Swedish hobbyists.
